Grégory Lazarian is an Associate Professor of Hematology. He teaches hematology at Sorbonne Paris Nord University Medical School.

He specializes in the molecular diagnosis of hematological malignancies at the Hematology Laboratory of Avicenne University Hospital. He is also in charge of the molecular biology platform dedicated to high-throughput sequencing.

His research focuses on understanding the interactions between chronic lymphocytic leukemia (CLL) cells and their microenvironment, as well as the impact of molecular abnormalities on disease progression and treatment resistance in CLL.
