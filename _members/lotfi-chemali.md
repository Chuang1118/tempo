Lotfi CHEMALI is a Clinical Assistant Professor with combined clinical and academic responsibilities within team SIMHEL of Inserm unit U1349. His expertise includes the diagnosis and follow-up of hematological malignancies using flow cytometry, as well as cytopathology.

His PhD project focuses on the interactions between chronic lymphocytic leukemia (CLL) cells and the tumor microenvironment, under the supervision of Prof. Fanny BARAN-MARSZAK.
